Learning Plan

What is my long term goal and/or career pathway?

- My long term goal is to eventually become a developer for a company that has projects that excite me and allow me to find satisfaction in my work.

A description of my own strengths and limitations in relation to my learning

- My strength to learning is that I believe anything is learnable for everyone if they put in the effort. I believe that I can learn anything given enough time and practice. My limitations is that sometimes I can be a bit to unfocused and get side tracked.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would I like to see developed in myself while at EDA?

- I want to learn the ability to become more of people person, I’ve always been reserved and shy but I want to be able to get to know more people.

A commitment to how I will manage my workload in this programme.

- I am committing to working well with others and just focus on my work. When things are building up and getting to me thenI will just take step back and let my diffuse mind solve the problem while I relax.

A commitment as to how and where I will seek help in a timely way

- I will seek help after like 2 days have passed of me trying, so I will make attempts then if I can’t solve it that day then I will take a break and do things that tend to be mundane or things that I already know. If the solution doesn’t come to me then I will ask for help.

A description of what I expect from the facilitation team

- My expectation is that they teach us best practice, or tell us things that we need improvement on and help me achieve on becoming the best developer that I can be.
