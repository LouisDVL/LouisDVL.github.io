Identity and Values

Explain a situation where you have had an ethical decision to make. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made and the consequences of the action you took.

- Every day I'm always faced with the decision of working towards my long term goals of becoming a software developer or playing video games all day instead. I always try to remember that I am doing this for my family and the future of my family, so when I look at it that way it is no longer a decision. The consequence of this decision is ofcourse that I improve at coding and I am just a little bit closer to my goal than I was yesterday.

Describe how your culture has influenced your values and identity.

- Me being Filipino it has always been family orientated and focused on your education and the betterment of the family. I believe that is what has driven me.

strengths and limitations in terms of my learning and career development.

Strengths:

- I am confident and I persevere within my learning, I know that sometimes it may take a while or it may not, but regardless I will learn, because I believe in myself.

- I always look for ways to improve, I read books like a Mind for Numbers by Barbara Oakley to improve my learning, Power of Habits by Charles Duhigg because I want to be the best version of myself, to not only offer to my future employers but also to the people around me.

Weaknesses:

- Because of my confidence to learn I am always seeking out knowledge and sometimes I become unfocused and have this insatiable desire for knowledge when I should be mastering a select few. Example of this is I should have been mastering JavaScript but got distracted by C#, and Python.

My strengths might help me in your learning journey and how they might intersect with learning obstacles.

- I believe my confidence and perseverance is the most helpful, because regardless of what happens I will keep trying and eventually I will succeed because the only way I fail is if I no longer try.
